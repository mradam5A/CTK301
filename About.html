About Me!

Hi my name is Maddy. I'm a student at Illinois State University studying to be a visual designer. I enjoy creating unique digital art and hope to be able to do that in some capacity when I enter the working world. Some things to know about me is that I am currently 19 years old. My love and interest in drawing, video games and technology brought me to digital art. Video Gaming has been a huge part of my recreational life and inspired me to start drawing digitally. I loved how my drawings looked after I was able to edit them. I want to make designs for companies and help them design and promote their product using all of my talent, experience and everything I have learned.
